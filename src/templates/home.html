hello! i'm muki. i'm a seattle native and a graduate from the university of washington with a bachelors in computer engineering.

i work on things across many domains that interest me. i love systems programming, low level languages, and functional programming. i've been loving zig recently. i'm also a motorsports fan who occasionally races a 1985 toyota mr2.

projects

ive worked on a lot of stuff and most of it is on github. a few that are interesting are below:

  • zzz, an open source micro framework for building networked/web services in zig. by focusing on minimizing allocations and utilizing modern system features (eg. io_uring), it can achieve very competitive performance. you can read more here. this website actually runs on zzz!
  • dandelion, a work-in-progress plug and play solution for active aerodynamics for motorsports. by providing an extensible and modular controller, more individual or small team racers can have access to active aerodynamics. you can read more here.
  • carter, an aftermarket cartridge for leapfrog devices that allow users to gain uart, microsd and root access to their devices. carter is open source, designed in kicad, and is currently available for purchase on kiboigo.com.
  • lost, an open source star tracker that i worked on during my time as the flight computer lead of the university of washington cubesat team.
